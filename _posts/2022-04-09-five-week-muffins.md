Yields 6 dozen

## Ingredients 

- 1 package raisin bran
- 5 cups flour
- 5 tsp baking soda
- 3 cups sugar
- 4 beaten eggs
- 1 cup oil
- 1 quart buttermilk

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together, cover tightly and store in fridge
2. Bake at 400&deg;F for 20 minutes as needed; never stir
