## Ingredients 

- 1 can pumpkin
- 1 can chicken broth
- 1/2 can coconut milk
- 1 1/2 tsp curry
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1-2 large portobello mushrooms
- salt/pepper to taste


## Directions

1. Saute mushrooms in butter
2. Add remaining ingredients
3. Simmer for 30 minutes
