## Ingredients 

- 3 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 2 tbs melted butter
- 1/4 tsp salt
- slices of bread


## Directions
1. Soak bread in mixture overnight, and turn several times
2. Place on buttery cookie sheet
3. Bake at 450&deg;F for 8 minutes
4. Turn and bake and additional 5-8  minutes
