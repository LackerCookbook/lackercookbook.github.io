## Ingredients 

- 1 head lettuce
- 1 cup celery
- 4 hard boiled eggs
- 10 oz frozen peas
- 1/2 cup green pepper
- 1/2 cup onion
- 8 slices bacon
- 2 cups mayonnaise
- 2 tbs sugar
- 4 oz cheese


## Directions

1. In a small dish mix together mayonnaise and sugar
2. In a large bowl add all ingredients and toss
3. Top with mayonnaise mixture and serve cold
