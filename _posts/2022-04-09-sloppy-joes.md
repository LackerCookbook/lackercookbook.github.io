## Ingredients 

- 1 lb hamburger
- 1/4 cup chopped onion
- salt/pepper
- 1 tbs powdered mustard
- 1 can chicken gumbo soup

## Directions

1. Fry hamburger and onions
2. Use a fork to separate meat
3. Add remaining ingredients and cook together until blended
