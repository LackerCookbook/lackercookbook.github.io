## Ingredients 

- 1/2 stick softened butter
- 4 or 5 oz Kraft Old English Cheese

### Spread
- 2 tbs mayo
- 1 can drained crabmeat


## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Spread onto 8 English Muffins
3. Bake at 450&deg;F for 10-15 minutes
