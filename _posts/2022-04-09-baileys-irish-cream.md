From Leslie and Tim

## Ingredients 

- 3 eggs
- 1 can eagle condensed milk
- 3 tbs Hershey's Syrup
- 3 tsp vanilla extract
- 3 tsp coconut extract
- 1 cup whiskey



## Directions

1. Blend for 3 minutes and refrigerate until cold
2. Add small amount of almond extract if desired
3. Keep Refrigerated
