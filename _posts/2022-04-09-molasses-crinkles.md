Makes 4 dozen.

## Ingredients 

- 1 cup brown sugar
- 3/4 cup shortening
- 1/4 cup molasses
- 1 egg
- 2 1/4 cup flour
- 2 tsp baking soda
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1 tsp ginger
- 1/2 tsp cloves

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients and separate into small balls
2. Bake at 375F until done
