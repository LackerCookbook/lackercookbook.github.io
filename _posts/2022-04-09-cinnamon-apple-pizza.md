## Ingredients 

- 1/2 cup oil
- 4 squares of baker's unsweetened melted chocolate
- 2 cups sugar
- 4 eggs
- 2 tsp vanilla
- 2 cups flour
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt


## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients together
2. Drop by the teaspoonful onto powdered sugar and shape into balls
3. Bake at 350&deg;F for 10-12 minutes
