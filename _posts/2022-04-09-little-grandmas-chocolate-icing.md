From Margaret Lacker

## Ingredients 

- 3/4 stick of butter
- 3 cups powdered sugar
- Splash of vanilla extract
- Splash of milk
- 4 heaping spoonfuls of cocoa

## Directions

1. Mix all ingredients with a beater until the right consistency is
reached and there are no lumps.
