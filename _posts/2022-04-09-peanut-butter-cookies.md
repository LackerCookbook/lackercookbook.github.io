## Ingredients 

- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup Crisco
- 1/2 cup peanut Butter
- 1 egg
- 1 1/4 cup flour
- 3/4 tsp baking soda
- 1/2 tsp baking powder



## Directions

1. Mix ingredients together
2. Refrigerate dough at 3&deg;F
3. Bake at 375&deg;F for 8-10 minutes or 10-12 minutes
